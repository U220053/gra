What is Earth Protocol?

+

Earth Protocol is an innovative blockchain solution designed to revolutionize decentralized finance. It provides secure, efficient, and sustainable financial services while prioritizing environmental consciousness.

How can I benefit from using $CoreUSD?

+

$CoreUSD offers stability, security, and seamless integration with the Earth Protocol ecosystem. Users can enjoy low transaction fees, fast settlements, and participate in various DeFi activities.

What is $CoreUSD?

+

$CoreUSD is a stablecoin within the Earth Protocol ecosystem, designed to maintain a 1:1 peg with the US Dollar. It enables users to transact and store value with minimal volatility.

Do you have a token?

+

Yes, the Earth Protocol has its native token, $EARTH, which serves as the governance token for the protocol and enables holders to participate in decision-making processes.
